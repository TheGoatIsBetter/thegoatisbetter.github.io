Project Wardog

Date: May 2022 - Present

Role: Project Lead | Lead Designer | Programmer

Type: Professional Project

Project Wardog is a Serious Management Puzzle Arcade game where you buy and sell to two sides of the same war, managing the tides in order to turn a profit. Day by day, delve into the depths of moral ambiguity, analyzing the media to decipher what’s real and what’s not. Be careful, as one wrong move could lead to your death, or worse, the death of your fortune…

Led a small team of around 10 at any one time, as Project Lead and Lead Designer, as well as wrote all drafts of the game design document. Successfully pitched game to UAT Game Studios, with plans to put the game up for Ukrainian relief charities.
